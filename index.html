Micah Lerner

I'm an engineer interested in maps, space, and futuristic ideas. I currently work on the Geo SRE team at Google. Previously, I helped build the Geospatial datasets powering Mapbox. Before that, I was a member of the infrastructure team at Strava, and a founding employee at Bitwise, the world's first cryptocurrency index fund.

For blog updates, find me on Twitter, subscribe to the email list, or add the Atom feed to your favorite RSS reader.
